Metaphor

A metaphore is a mapping from one domain to another.For example, moo is to cow as beep is to car. These mappings get so buried in our language that we don't even notice their effects.

For example,take the language used to descirbe a debate:

  • "he shot down her ideas."

  • "she defended her position."

  • "she overwhelmed his arguments."
These are all debate-as-war metaphors.It's a zero sum game.Someone wins and some loses. The idea that both people could learn from each other just doen't come up
